Restoration business opportunity — FAQ

Is this a franchise?

No. Respro grows through operator-owners and structured business partnerships — not franchises. Ownership where the law allows it; partnership structures built to each state's requirements elsewhere.

What services would my territory provide?

24/7 water damage restoration, fire & smoke damage restoration, mold remediation, air duct and dryer vent cleaning, Apollo-UV air purification, and the OXenate™ ozone program.

Do I need restoration experience?

It helps, but it's not required — operators train to one national standard. Our people come from restoration, trades, insurance, adjusting, sales leadership, and business ownership.

How do I get customers?

The national 24/7 line — 1-888-RESTOR-U — routes your territory's calls to your team, backed by the national brand.
