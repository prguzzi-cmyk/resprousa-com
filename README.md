## Legal note (before heavy promotion)
The "Partner With Us" section describes territory operators (CPs/RVPs/agents) and
"business partnerships — not franchises." Franchise law looks at substance, not
labels: if partners pay fees for a brand + system, several states may treat it as
a franchise regardless of wording. Have counsel review this page's copy and the
actual partnership agreements before scaling recruitment.
